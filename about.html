Aloha!

I'm a healthcare professional with a background in clinical laboratory science. I have collaborated with cross-functional teams to develop strategies to make routine operations quicker and simpler for employees, resulting in reduced lab errors and improved patient care. I was immediately excited when I learned about UX/UI design because it would allow me to brainstorm solution-focused strategies and ignite my creative side through design.

My solution-driven and creative personality have motivated me to obtain my UX/UI design certification from the University of Denver. I want to work for a company that holds great company values and offers a service that has a quality impact on people.

I am confident that I will offer you the grit and zest that you are seeking in a UX/UI designer.

Data Synthesis & Analysis

I always start a problem with a research plan to identify the pain point before starting any design work. It has helped me significantly in my work to create a personable and intuitive design for users.

User Empathy

Based on research findings, I create a solid persona that helps me and my team understand users' needs, wants, experiences, behavior, and goals. This sticks with us through the design process to keep the eye on the prize to relieve our user's from a specific pain point.

Interaction Design

Develop engaging and intuitive interfaces to enhance user's interaction with the product. My designs are motivated so that users are able to obtain the feeling of accomplishment and satisfaction easily and quickly.
